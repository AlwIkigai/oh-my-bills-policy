# Privacy Policy – Oh, My Bills

**Effective Date:** 2026-02-04

Oh, My Bills (“the App”) is designed to be private, offline-first, and respectful of your personal data.

## Data Collection
Oh, My Bills does not collect, transmit, or store personal data on any external servers.

All data you enter (such as cash amounts and bills) is stored locally on your device only.

## Data Sharing
The App does not share your data with third parties.
No analytics, advertising, or tracking services are used.

## Internet Usage
The App does not require an internet connection to function for its core features.
The app may contact Expo servers only when you choose to check for updates. No personal or financial data is sent.

## Financial Disclaimer
Oh, My Bills does not provide financial advice.
All calculations are informational only.
